The extent of the destruction is becoming more and more clear as the floodwaters recede. The Most Devastating Flood in Pakistan

What is happening?

The terrible storms, floods, and landslides brought on by this year's high monsoon rains in Pakistan have affected almost 33 million people, including roughly 16 million children. A number of significant rivers burst their banks, and dams flooded, damaging houses, farms, and essential infrastructure including roads, bridges, schools, hospitals, and public health institutions in addition to residences and other buildings.
